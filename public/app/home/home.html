HKA Trello App
Custom interfaces for the Trello system used by the HKA team in managing projects. Click the menu button at the upper right to open the naviagion system and interact with the following tools:

Card Printer

Simple report allowing you to select any/all cards in a board and produce a printable .doc file.

Checklist To Cards

Functionality within a Trello card to convert one or more checklist items to cards with links maintained to/from the original checklist item.

Milestones

Maintain a trello list as your "milestone schedule". Once a list is identified in the app settings, the cards on that list will have extra functionality to set and display milestone status and key dates.

Risk Register

Create cards in a "risk" enabled list and produce reports in a risk register format.

Sprint Reporting

Quick and easy sprint status report.

Freshdesk Ticketing

Ability to request Freshdesk tickets from a card and maintain a link from the card to the generated Freshdesk ticket.

Security Requirements Tracking

Ability to define basic security configuration requirements for defined groups and add notes for additional security needs.

Resource Management

Ability to track resource assignment on board and review all resource utilization across the Trello team.
(Feature is only accesible to members of the team.)
