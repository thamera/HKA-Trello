The HKA Trello App provides custom interfaces for the Trello system used by the HKA team in managing projects. Click the menu button at the upper right to open the naviagion system and interact with the following tools:

  • Card Printer

    Simple report allowing you to select any/all cards in a board and produce a printable .doc file.

  • Sprint Reporting

    Quick and easy sprint status report.


Latest Enhancements/Updates

  • V 2.0.0 - 1/9/2018:
    1. Enhancement: Redesigned as a Trello Power-Up. The chrome extension is no longer necessary and the app is now browser indepentent.
    2. Enhancement: Schedule Milestones are now individual cards located within a milestone list. New fields added to Trello to capture milestone data.
    3. Update: Checklist to Cards has been relocated to the card interface. To convert checklist to cards, open a card with the checklists to be converted and select the option in the trello card tools menu.

Attachments and cc's cand be added before submitting the new ticket request. The created ticket will then be automatically linked back onto the Trello card (see next item regarding Freshdesk links).
    5. Enhancement: When attaching a url to a card that is a Freshdesk Ticket URL, PMWeb will render the ticket information from Freshdesk on the Trello card including attachments and the most recent conversation history.
